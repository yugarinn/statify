About

This is just a simple webapp to get a nice overview of your personal user stats accesible via the Spotify API. Once you click on the "Check stats" button you'll be redirectioned to Spotify to login into your account (unless you're already logged in the browser) and right after that you should see all of your personal stats. Kinda cool.

It's not associated with Spotify by any means.
